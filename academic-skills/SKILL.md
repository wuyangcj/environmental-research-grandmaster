# Academic Research & Scholarship Skills

## Overview
This skill focuses on the foundational pillars of academia: rigor, integrity, and methodology.

## 1. Citation & Reference Management
- **Styles**: Proficiency in APA, MLA, IEEE, Chicago, Vancouver, and Nature styles.
- **Tools**: Integration with Zotero/BibTeX patterns.
- **Verification**: Always cross-check DOIs and verify that citations in text match the reference list.

## 2. Literature Review & Synthesis
- **Thematic Synthesis**: Grouping literature by themes/arguments rather than author-by-author.
- **Gap Analysis**: Explicitly identifying what is *missing* in current literature.
- **Critical Thinking**: Evaluating the methodology of cited works (sample size, bias, controls).

## 3. Academic Integrity
- **Plagiarism Prevention**: Ensuring original phrasing and proper attribution.
- **Self-Correction**: Identifying potential conflicts of interest or overstatements in research claims.
